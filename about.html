Our Story

Connecting government needs with innovative, dependable solutions.

Who We Are

The Siddiq Group is a Virginia-based government contracting firm, founded by two brothers with expertise in information technology, finance, and procurement. We bring a modern, agile approach to acquiring goods and services for federal, state, and local agencies.

Built on a legacy of federal acquisition, we prioritize accountability, transparency, and timely delivery. As a minority-owned small business pursuing SWaM certification, we use forward-thinking strategies to deliver measurable value to our government partners.

Our mission: Build trust, exceed expectations, and help agencies deliver better public service. One contract at a time.

Our Goal

At Siddiq Group, our goal is to empower government agencies with procurement solutions that are fast, compliant, and cost-effective without compromising quality. We bridge the gap between agency requirements and reliable suppliers, ensuring every contract delivers measurable value.

By combining deep industry knowledge, advanced technology, and a commitment to excellence, we streamline acquisition processes, reduce risk, and help our partners serve the public more effectively.
