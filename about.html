Our Story

Building bridges between government needs and exceptional solutions

Siddiq Group is a Virginia-based firm specializing in the acquisition of goods and services for government agencies. Founded by two brothers with complementary backgrounds in information technology, finance, and procurement, we represent a new generation of entrepreneurs bringing a modern, agile approach to government contracting.

With roots in a family legacy of federal acquisition, we understand the critical importance of accountability, transparency, and timely delivery. Our goal is to deliver the same standard of professionalism and reliability to the state and local levels.

As a minority-owned small business actively pursuing SWaM certification, we are proud to reflect the strength of our heritage and the promise of innovation. Our diverse perspective and dedication to excellence position us to streamline procurement processes and provide lasting value to our government partners.
